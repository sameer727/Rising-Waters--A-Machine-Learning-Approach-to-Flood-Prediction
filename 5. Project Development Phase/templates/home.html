System Context & Database Schema

Target Predictors

The system digests ten core hydrological and climate features for prediction:

  • Annual Rainfall (mm): Total yearly precipitation metrics.
  • Seasonal Rainfall (mm): Monsoon or seasonal cumulative precipitation.
  • Cloud Cover (%): Portion of sky covered by clouds.
  • Humidity (%): Relative air moisture index.
  • Temperature (°C): Mean ambient temperature reading.
  • River Water Level (m): Stage height of local rivers.
  • Drainage Capacity (%): Runoff capacity effectiveness.
  • Water Flow (cumecs): Discharge rate of surface water flow.
  • Reservoir Level (%): Current capacity of reservoirs.
  • Soil Moisture (%): Volumetric water content in soil.

Prediction Pipeline Flow

Our machine learning pipeline enforces rigorous preprocessing and evaluation:

  • Feature Inputs: Captured via UI or real-time climate telemetry.
  • Feature Scaling: Normalized using a pre-trained StandardScaler.
  • Model Architecture: Evaluated with ensemble algorithms optimized for extreme weather indicators.
  • Trigger Alert: Redirects to warning status page with instant safety drills and advice.
